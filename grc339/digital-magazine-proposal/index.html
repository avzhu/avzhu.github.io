Introduction

19th (時久)

19 in Chinese is 十九 (shi2jiu3), which has the same pronunciation as 時久. 時久 is a combination of the phrases 時常 (shi2chang2), meaning duration, and 永久 (yong2jiu3), meaning permanence, to represent the everlasting nature of the hanfu. Our magazine strives to bring awareness to the evolution of hanfu fashion and its cultural significance.
